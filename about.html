Michael Santos was born in Germany, on December 21st 1977.

Being a Portuguese descendant, he moves to Portugal with his family when he was only one year old. In Portugal, he studies and carries out compulsory military service.

In high school Michael took a class in photography and joined the photography club. He fell in love with the whole process from capturing fleeting moments to discovering different processes in developing film. He also became fascinated with vintage cameras and started collecting, fixing and using them. With his new passion around photography he is compelled to go back to Germany in 2003 to achieve a degree for Photography Design in Stuttgart.
